01 Customer Intelligence

Referral Quality Analysis for a Neobank

Analysed 6,393 referrals across two bonus tiers. Card activation and inviter quality drive retention far more than bonus size. Modelled policy changes that cut cost per active customer by 33%.

02 Growth Modelling

Measuring Subscription Growth From Imperfect Data

Assessed six source tables for a D2C pet food company, designed a medallion architecture, and calculated 11% QoQ growth from a 10,000-subscription base. Product fit drives 48% of voluntary churn.

03 Revenue Operations

Diagnosed a pipeline breakdown across 33,076 leads for an HR SaaS platform after a 3,270% volume spike. Isolated capacity, data quality, and qualification causes, then delivered a 30/60/90 day plan.

About me

My route into data was not a straight line. It started in accounting and management, wound through customer, revenue, and business operations, and landed in data analytics. I began on the ground floor in client support and implementation, learning how a business really works from the inside: what customers need, where processes break, and how frontline reality differs from the dashboard leadership sees. That ground-level fluency is what makes my analysis land.

Across tech and not-for-profits, the work I care about most connects numbers to decisions: analysis that reframes how a room thinks, requirements that save months of rework, and data stories that shift something real for the business.

I'm a Melbourne local and a Startmate Women's Fellow, and I firmly believe rigorous analysis and warm working relationships sit on the same side of a great team. Always happy to connect, compare notes, or grab a (virtual) coffee.
